India's fashion landscape stretches far beyond what mainstream platforms show you. From the block-printed cottons of Bagru to the silk weaves of Kanchipuram, from the mirror-worked ghagras of Kutch to the indigo-dyed fabrics of Bagh, every region carries centuries of craft and living tradition. Yet the artisans behind this work remain invisible to buyers outside their home districts.

NotWhat bridges that gap. We built a marketplace where regional fashion sellers, small boutiques, and independent artisans upload shoppable video reels showcasing their products in real context, not just on a white background. Buyers discover India's diversity through a feed curated to their taste, and go from watching a reel to completing a purchase in seconds.

Whether you are a buyer looking to wear something that carries a story, or a seller who wants to reach customers beyond your city, NotWhat is built for you. Launching on iOS first, with Android to follow.

Refund and Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2025

PLACEHOLDER: Replace with counsel-reviewed text before launch.

Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled within a set window of placing the order, provided the seller has not yet dispatched the item. To cancel, go to My Orders in the app and tap Cancel Order.

Refunds

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within a specified number of business days of the return being accepted by the seller.

Non-Refundable Items

  • Custom or made-to-order garments
  • Items marked as Final Sale at the time of purchase
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered after delivery

Return Policy

Last updated: June 2025

PLACEHOLDER: Replace with counsel-reviewed text before launch.

Return Window

Returns are accepted within a specified number of days of delivery, subject to the individual seller's return policy as displayed on the product page.

Conditions for Return

  • Item must be unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging
  • All original tags must be intact and attached
  • Return request must be raised through the app within the return window

Return Process

Raise a return request in My Orders. Our logistics partner will schedule a pickup. Once the item is received and inspected, your refund will be processed within the specified business days.

Shipping Policy

Last updated: June 2025

PLACEHOLDER: Replace with counsel-reviewed text before launch.

Delivery Timelines

Standard delivery within India takes a variable number of business days depending on the seller's location and your delivery pincode.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are set by individual sellers and displayed on the product page and at checkout. Orders above a threshold amount may qualify for free shipping where the seller has enabled it.

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via SMS and push notification. Live tracking is available in My Orders at any time.

International Shipping

NotWhat currently ships within India only. International availability will be announced when it becomes available.
